# 🚀 Advanced Chat Features - Quick Start Guide

Welcome to TalkDude's advanced chat features! Here's everything you need to know.

---

## 📱 How to Use Each Feature

### 1️⃣ Delete Messages

**Want to remove a message you sent?**

#### On Computer:
1. **Right-click** on your message
2. Choose:
   - 🗑️ **Delete for Me** - Only you won't see it
   - 🗑️ **Delete for Everyone** - Nobody will see it

#### On Phone/Tablet:
1. **Press and hold** the message (about half a second)
2. Menu pops up
3. Tap your delete option

**What happens?**
- "Delete for Me" → Message stays for others
- "Delete for Everyone" → Shows "*This message was deleted*" for everyone

---

### 2️⃣ Reply to Messages

**Need to respond to a specific message?**

#### On Computer:
1. **Right-click** the message
2. Click **Reply** 
3. Type your response above the preview
4. Hit send!

#### On Phone/Tablet:
**Option A - Swipe:**
1. **Swipe left** on the message
2. Reply box appears
3. Type and send

**Option B - Long Press:**
1. **Hold** the message
2. Tap **Reply**
3. Type and send

**To cancel reply:**
- Tap the **X** button in the preview

---

### 3️⃣ One-Time View Photos 📸

**Send photos that disappear after viewing!**

#### Sending:
1. Tap the **+** button
2. Pick your photo
3. Select **"One-Time View"** option
4. Send - it shows as a special card

#### Receiving:
1. You'll see a purple/blue card saying "VIEW ONCE"
2. **Tap the card** to open
3. Photo fills your screen
4. You have **10 seconds** to view it
5. **Tap anywhere** or wait to close
6. **Gone forever!** ✨

**⚠️ Important:**
- You can only view it **ONCE**
- **Screenshot attempts are detected** and reported to sender
- Can't save or share this photo
- Disappears from chat after viewing

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Quick Delete**: On mobile, long press is faster than navigating menus
2. **Reply Context**: The reply preview shows who you're replying to - double check before sending!
3. **One-Time Safety**: Only send sensitive photos as one-time view
4. **Accidental Delete?**: Too late! Make sure before you click
5. **Swipe Sensitivity**: Swipe gently - too fast might not register

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I recover a deleted message?**  
A: Nope! Once deleted, it's gone for good.

**Q: Will someone know if I screenshot their one-time photo?**  
A: Yes! They get notified immediately.

**Q: Can I reply to deleted messages?**  
A: No, deleted messages can't be replied to.

**Q: Do one-time photos work in group chats?**  
A: Currently only in 1-on-1 chats.

**Q: Can I edit a reply after sending?**  
A: Not yet. Check your message before sending!

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**Menu not appearing?**
- Try clicking directly on the message bubble
- Make sure you're not in a thread view

**Swipe not working?**
- Swipe horizontally, not diagonally
- Don't swipe too fast
- Try long press instead

**Photo won't open?**
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the chat page
- The photo may have already been viewed

**Can't delete a message?**
- You can only delete your own messages
- Some old messages may have time limits
